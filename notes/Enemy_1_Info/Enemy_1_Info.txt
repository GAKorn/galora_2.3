The enemy for the intro stage.
Aside from it, the level will only have a boss fight.

Shape:
- A warrior with some sort of hispanic/indian (some like Mayans or Incas) skin color.
- It features a spear and armor in the style of the roman legion.

Behavior
- Idle: Stands a bit.
- Walk: It's patrolling an area.
- Attack: attacks the player with some anticipation frames
- Hurt: suffer damage from the player attacks
- Death: when HP <= 0, death animation plays

Behavioral change
Walk a certai area > Stops in idle for 1 second > Walks certain area in the oposite direction > Repeat
Sees the player: walks towards the player.
Player in range: attacks
